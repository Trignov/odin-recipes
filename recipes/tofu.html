Spicy Teriyaki Tofu

Description

An excellent recipe that will excite your taste buds, while keeping you strong and healthy! This meal is easy to make on any weekday night after you come home from work and need to feed the whole family. Be sure to bookmark this one so you can come back and whip up a new batch every week!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz. Tofu
  • Teriyaki Sauce
  • Neutral Oil
  • Sliced Ginger
  • Corn Starch
  • Scallions
  • Coriander
  • Sambal Ooleek

Steps

  1. Remove Tofu from packaging and drain excess water.
  2. Slice Tofu into full length strips and coat in corn starch.
  3. Deep-fry tofu until pale golden.
  4. Stir-fry teriyaki sauce with sambal ooleek, ginger, and tofu.
  5. Toss in scallions and coriander garnishing and serve.
